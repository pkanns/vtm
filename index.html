The Problem We're Solving

Most work management tools track tasks.
We track the work behind the work.

There is a gap between assigning something and understanding how it was delivered. That gap is where accountability lives, where growth happens, and where most platforms stop.

Mulai is built around the gig — a discrete, trackable unit of work with a lead, a doer, a start, and a finish. Every gig carries its own evaluation. Every hour logged is attached to something real. The structure is simple. The discipline it creates is not.

We are not building another project management tool. We are building a reflection engine — one that makes the quality of work visible over time, across people, across projects.

The Gig Lifecycle

Three real steps.

From the moment work is defined to the moment it is understood.

01
Place

A gig is created — given a code, a title, a project context, a lead and a doer. If it's recurring, the schedule is set once and runs automatically. If it's urgent, it's placed and started immediately.

02
Deliver

The doer works. Time is logged — automatically with GPS on clock-in, or manually after the fact. The gig moves from open to in progress. Nothing is lost. Everything is timestamped.

03
Reflect

Both parties rate the work across two dimensions — engagement and effectiveness. The final score is the lead's weighted view. Honest. Structured. On the record. This is where most platforms stop. This is where Mulai begins.

What's Built

Principles made concrete.

Everything below is live, in production, being used today.

Structure
Projects & Gigs

A two-level hierarchy. Projects provide context. Gigs are the unit of work. Each gig gets an auto-generated code — MULAI_GIG_O_001 — that encodes its project, category, type and sequence.

Accountability
Lead & Doer Model

Every gig has a Lead who holds the standard and a Doer who delivers. Role-based access means each person sees exactly what is relevant to them — nothing more, nothing less.

Evaluation
Structured Reflection

Two dimensions — Engagement and Effectiveness — each with weighted attributes. The Doer self-rates. The Lead rates. The final score is the Lead's view. Rewards and pricing are a future module. The reflection stands alone.

Time
Auto & Manual Tracking

Clock in with a button. GPS captured automatically. Clock out when done — duration calculated, location recorded. Manual entry for everything else. A clean personal log, always available.

Recurrence
Scheduled Work

Recurring gigs run on a schedule — weekly, fortnightly, monthly. A GitHub Actions cron creates each instance automatically, carries the rover forward, respects end dates and stop flags.

Security
Auth & Session Guard

Supabase authentication. Role-based page guards. Idle timeout with warning at 28 minutes. Session cleared on sign-out. Every page protected. Built to be trusted by the people who use it.
